Renovation costs in Budapest 2025

📅 February 2025 ⏱ 6 min read 📍 Budapest

The most common question we receive is: how much will my renovation cost? The honest answer is: it depends. But in this article we break down the real unit costs by trade, so you can build a realistic budget before your first meeting with us.

Base costs per m²

All prices below are indicative for 2025 and include labour and standard materials. Quality multipliers apply: economy (×0.7), standard (×1.0), premium (×1.5), luxury (×2.2).

  • Partial renovation (cosmetic works only): 35,000–55,000 HUF/m²
  • Full renovation (all installations replaced): 65,000–110,000 HUF/m²
  • New construction: 120,000–200,000 HUF/m²

Key trades: what drives costs

Electrical installation (full rewire): 12,000–18,000 HUF/m². Plumbing: 15,000–22,000 HUF/m². Heating: 18,000–27,000 HUF/m². Floor finishing (laminate): 6,000–9,000 HUF/m²; (parquet): 22,000–33,000 HUF/m².

"Kitchen and bathrooms are the highest-cost items per m². A standard kitchen fit-out starts at 300,000 HUF; a premium kitchen can exceed 2,700,000 HUF. A standard bathroom fit-out starts at 180,000 HUF."

VAT and additional costs

All prices above are net of VAT. In Hungary, the standard VAT rate on construction services is 27% — one of the highest in the EU. This adds significantly to the total budget and should be factored in from the start.
